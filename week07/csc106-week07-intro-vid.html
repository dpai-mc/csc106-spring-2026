Weeks 7 & 8 — Introduction

Welcome to the final stretch of the course. Week 7 is the most hands-on week yet: you will configure your Ubuntu Server with a static IP address, a running web server, a firewall, a scheduled monitoring task, and SSH key authentication. Week 8 brings everything together with Mini-Project 2, where you build a brand-new server from scratch and document the full process.
